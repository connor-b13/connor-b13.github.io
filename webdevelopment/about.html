About me

Hello! My name is Connor Bailey, and I am a Computer Science student with a passion for software development, problem solving, and new technologies. Coming from a small town taught me the impact a single person can have on the lives of others. That experience shaped my goals today. My biggest interest is in software engineering, machine learning, and the development of new technologies that improve the way people live, work and connect with one another. Through my academic studies and personal projects, I have gained experience designing algorithms, building software solutions, analyzing data, and solving complex technical problems. Outside of my professional life and passion projects, I am compelled by a desire to see things change. Most mornings, I can be found in the gym, where one of my favorite hobbies is improving my body. On top of that, I love to take long trips in the wilderness, and challenge myself with difficult video-games. I believe that these interests exemplify my enjoyment of undertaking large challenges and seeing them through.
